Privately Copenhagen. An intimate retreat

The Hotel KEAlifornia is both a landmark & a luxury retreat, beloved and cherished by visitors. Get carried away by Copenhagen bubbly lifestyle.

The charm of a private mansion

Each 45 bedrooms and 7 suites preserve the spirit of the place whilst updating it once again to suit today’s styles. The interior designer has embraced the Hotel KEAlifornia historic aesthetic and married it effortlessly with contemporary comfort.

Free Parking & Free Charging Station

We are offering booked parking spots for any of our guests and 25 booked charging stations as well as disabled-friendly services with companion upon request.

Breakfast included in the deal

Hotel KEAlifornia has an evening restaurant and a café. The charming restaurant serves seasonal menus of the day as well as delicious à la carte dishes featuring fresh raw ingredients prepared with the utmost care and affection.

Wellness facilities

Don’t miss out on Hotel KEAfornia’s unique Spa & Wellness area with indoor and outdoor facilities, including a large roof terrace spa. Pamper yourself with one of the wellness area’s many treatment options and wide range of beauty products. Enjoy the relaxing atmosphere in the hall or a drink in the cosy lobby bar.

We Are Reliable

Free Wifi

We are offering our guests a good quality internet connection available in the entire hotel free of charge.

Pets

Pets welcome upon request. There may be additional fees.

Public Transportation

We have got a train station as well as a Metro station near our hotel (5 Km). You can as well take the booked shuffle bus to the city center just in front of the Hotel. The airport is situated at 15 Km from our location.

Weekly Mini-Golf tournaments

If you are an active person and a long-stay guest you can join our weekly mini-golf marathons for booked!

How to find us?

We are located very centrally, in one of the most known neighbourhood in Copenhagen. You can access us by taking train, bus or bike.
